About Project

SMART UV EXPOSURE ALERT SYSTEM

A capstone-style environmental safety dashboard combining ESP32 Bluetooth data, VEML6030 local brightness readings, weather UV context, and alert recommendations.

Hardware

  • ESP32 development board
  • VEML6030 ambient visible light sensor
  • OLED display
  • RGB LEDs and buzzer
  • Bluetooth Low Energy communication

Important Sensor Note

The VEML6030 measures ambient brightness in lux. It does not directly measure UV. The dashboard estimates UV risk by combining lux trends with weather UV and daylight data.

Purpose

The system helps demonstrate local environmental monitoring, real-time device feedback, and weather-aware safety recommendations without WiFi hardware communication.
